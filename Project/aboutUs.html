About Us

Who We Are

Admaro Limited is a full service agency providing one-stop-shop for all aspect of marketing communication and promotion to clients across a variety of market sectors.

We are a team of experts providing cutting edge business and marketing communication solutions to businesses, organizations, institutions etc. We offer a complete range of services from strategic planning, marketing research, advertising, data driven media buying, creative designs, commercial production, sales promotion, to event management. We bring our extensive experience to the table in working for clients in many different industries and work to offer business solutions and campaigns which are tailored to the specific needs of any business. Our ability to customize business and marketing communication solutions to our clients allows us to work as an extension of their own marketing departments. We have a good track record of creating effective campaigns which help our clients to meet their business objectives – which is why Admaro Limited has established so many long lasting business relationships with its clients.

Vision

To build position-trusted brand for both local and international clients with result-oriented advertising, strategic marketing planning, and total marketing support; and become a nationally-recognized leader in the field of communication and marketing.

Mission

To become one of Ghana’s top 10 busin Marketing Communication companies through the provision of strategy, marketing communication solutions and outstanding customer service.

Objective

To build a highly motivated team of professionals who would be capable of implementing the company’s vision to building position-trusted brands for clients
To become Ghana’s leading independent media buying company in terms of billings and reputation for integrity.
To provide total marketing communication solution to advertisers and set the lowest media floor price in the industry.
To improve the quality of our service activities to ensure total customer satisfaction.

Core Values

We would always put our customers first
